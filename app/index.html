Mite Mitreski
Mite Mitreski currently works at Tricode and has experience in various open source technologies, with primary focus on Java and JVM. He has also worked as a Oracle certifed java trainer in local educational center and as custom solutions developer at Netcetera as well as GenrepSoft in the past. He is deeply involved in activities sounding Java User Group Macedonia, free and open source software, open data formats and the open web. Occasonaly he writes at his blog and twitter @mitemitreski Recently he co-author a book titled HTML 5 Data and Services Cookbook.
Pance Cavkovski
Pance Cavkovski is a software engineer working at Netcetera in Skopje, Macedonia. As part of the team, he has specialized working in Java, Javascript, Flex/ActionScript and .NET technologies. The scopes of the projects he has worked so far have varied from CAD application, simulations and visualizations to finance applications, distributed web systems and infrastructures. He is known for the CodeFu system which he redesigned, developed in most and is one of the head administrators. As of 2009 he is an active member of the Macedonian Java User Group.
